ABOUT ASCII

Assosication for Students of Computer science for Information Interchange is a club exclusive to the department of Computer Science and Engineering. It's a student driven club. We conduct competitions in gaming and coding and we do conduct workshops. We conduct talk shows where people from various feilds talk about their field and give an insight on that particular feild. From this club you get a huge exposure and learning oppurtunities in terms of organising an event and people showcase their talents in the feild they like.
Last year we conducted 5 events and a talk given by the MIT alumnus Dr. Prakash Govindan. Last year was successful. We hope this year will be successful too.
